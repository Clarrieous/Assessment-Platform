Pre-Assessment Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before proceeding with your assessment:

  1. Ensure that your system meets the compatibility requirements.
  2. Find a quiet space free from distractions.
  3. Phones and other electronic devices are not allowed during the assessment.
  4. Have a valid form of ID ready to present if required.
  5. Complete the assessment in one sitting; no breaks will be allowed.
  6. Review the questions carefully before submitting your answers.
  7. If you experience any technical issues, notify the proctor immediately.

Good luck with your assessment!
